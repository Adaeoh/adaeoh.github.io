Hi, I'm Liam.

I am a photographer and web-designer based in Sydney, Australia.

I have been following my passion for photography & filmmaking since 2017 and started practicing web-design in 2020. I entered Western Sydney University as an undergraduate student in 2020, studying in a Diploma of Communications & Bachelor of Screen Media (Arts Prod). I have utilised my photography and filmmaking skills in a variety of areas outside of University such as through my involvement as an event photographer at the Sydney Matsuri Japan Festival and St Mark's Catholic College during 2018 and 2019.

I use Adobe Creative Cloud programs such as Lightroom, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and After Effects to edit and publish my works to my social media pages - examples of my works are available in my 'showcase' - links to my social media are available in through 'contact'.

I have hopes to possibly start up my own freelance photography business in the future.

2021 Liam Adao.
